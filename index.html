About the Model

🧠

Architecture

Built on RoBERTa Base (125M parameters), fine-tuned with a custom 3-class classification head. Trained with AdamW optimizer, linear warmup scheduler, mixed-precision FP16, and label smoothing.

📦

Training Data

Fine-tuned on 200,000+ samples from:
• IMDB movie reviews
• Stanford SST-2 sentences
• Twitter Tweet Eval dataset
• Yelp restaurant reviews

🎯

What It Detects

Positive: Satisfaction, enthusiasm, praise
Neutral: Facts, balance, mixed signals
Negative: Frustration, criticism, anger
Works on reviews, tweets & comments.
